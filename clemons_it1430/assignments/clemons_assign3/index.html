About us

Magic Kingdom is located at the Walt Disney World Resort in Lake Buena Vista, just outside of Orlando. It is my favorite park because it is where all my favorite movies and shows come to life. Whether it's seeing the iconic Cinderella Castle or racing through the stars on Space Mountain, the park is filled with the kind of magic and wonder that makes it the "Most Magical Place on Earth." Beyond the rides and attractions, the atmosphere at Magic Kingdom is what truly makes it special. The park is filled with the sights, sounds, and smells of Disney magic, from the cheerful music to the delicious aroma of popcorn and churros. The cast members are always friendly and eager to help, adding to the overall sense of enchantment. Whether it's meeting beloved characters or watching a parade go by, every moment at Magic Kingdom feels like stepping into a fairy tale.
